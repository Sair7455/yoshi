About The Ride

Who We Are

Jawa, a historic motorcycle brand, has made a comeback in India under Classic Legends, with models like the Jawa 42, 42 Bobber, and Perak. The Jawa 42 is a modern retro bike available in various colors and variants, powered by a 294.72cc engine.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: The Jawa motorcycle brand, known for its classic designs and rich history, aims to blend retro aesthetics with modern engineering. The company's vision is to offer motorcycles that evoke a sense of nostalgia while providing a reliable and enjoyable riding experience
